Agriculture is the backbone of our economy and society, but it's also a source

The Farmer Assistant Application seamlessly integrates cutting-edge technology to revolutionize agricultural practices. This multifaceted tool employs advanced algorithms for crop disease recognition, features an interactive chatbot for real-time agricultural assistance, conducts eligibility assessments for government schemes, and utilizes image recognition for efficient weed detection, empowering farmers with comprehensive support for optimal crop management.

Crop Disease Recognition

Crop disease recognition utilizes the combined power of Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs) and Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) to enhance precision in identifying and diagnosing plant diseases, revolutionizing agricultural management

Chatbot

Lamini LLM-powered question answering chatbot for farmers seamlessly provides tailored and accurate agricultural insights, offering real-time assistance to address a wide range of queries and empower farmers with valuable information

Government Scheme Eligibility

A Naive Bayes classifier applied to government scheme eligibility for farmers accurately determines the specific schemes they qualify for, streamlining the identification process based on key criteria and enhancing accessibility to crucial agricultural support initiatives.

Weed Detection

Weed detection leverages the synergy of Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs) and You Only Look Once (YOLO) object detection, enhancing the efficiency and accuracy of identifying and localizing weeds in agricultural fields.
